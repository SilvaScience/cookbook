Frangipane is a classic almond flavored paste used in patisserie like croissant d’amandes and Gallet de Rois to create a rich

Ingredients
	- Salted butter, 125 g, room temperature
	- Sugar, 125 g
	- Poudre D’amandes, 150 g
	- 3 large eggs, room temperature
	- Vanila extract to taste
 	- Salt to taste

Steps:
 	- In a high walled bowl, combine the butter and sugar. Beat on high speed until no visible grains of sugar remain. Add salt and vanilla as well
	- One at a time, beat in eggs at medium speed. The subsequent egg should only be added after the butter and eggs have fully emulsified. 
	- At low speed, beat in almond powder/flour slowly.
	- Once fully incorporated, mix on high speed until a whipped texture is achieved.

Store in a pastry bag in the fridge until ready for use in desired baked good. Allow to return to room tempt before piping, at least 5 minutes on the counter. Store in air tight container in the freezer if excess remains.
