Ingredients:
- Dates (Mejhoul are recommended but any will work)
- Goat cheese
- Optionnal: Bacon (Trash american version)

Recipe:
1- Slice the dates open and remove the pits from the dates
2- Stuff them with goat cheese, make sure they still close
3- Wrap in bacon (Optionnal)
4- Put in oven at 375 F for about 20 minutes
5- Serve and enjoy
