CONGRI RICE
1-rice (2 pounds)
2-red bean (1 pound)
3-chilli (1 unit)
4-Onion (1 unit) 
5-garlic cloves (3 units)
6-a little salt
7-a little sugar
8-two tablespoons of fat
9-six cups of water
10-half tablespoon cumin
11-half tablespoon oregano
12-pork in pieces (half pound)

1-The beans should be washed and soaked in water with a chili bell pepper and a crushed clove of garlic since the night before or at least some hours before.
2-Then, they are cooked in the same soaking water until they are soft.
3-Then, separate the water from the beans and measure a liter of water, supplementing it if necessary.
4-In a frying pan, fry the pork rinds until they release their fat, adding the cumin, oregano, onion, finely chopped chili bell pepper and garlic.
5-Add the beans, bean water, salt and sugar.
6-Once it starts to boil, add the previously washed rice and let it cook over medium heat with the pot well covered until it is ready.
