Trash (snack) recipe
Henry Kantrow
July 24, 2024

Ingredients:
1 box crispix
0.5 bag pretzel sticks
1.5 cup pecans
1 stick butter
2 tbsp lea & perrin
garlic salt
onion salt
chili powder
red pepper
lemon juice
celery salt

Steps:
1. Melt butter and add Lea & Perrin and spices
2. Pour over crispix and pecans
3. In roasting pan, cook at 225 F for 45 min - 1.5 hrs until browned
4. Allow to cool, then bag.
