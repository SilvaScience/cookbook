Recipe for Sospwanwa

Ingredients (quantities are up for taste):
- Black beans (uncooked)
- Salted Pork lard
- Onion
- Garlic
- All spice/Nutmeg/Bay leaves
- Olive oil

Steps:
- Soak the black beans overnight and the salted lard in separate bowls. THe amount of water you use to soak the lard changes how salty the recipe will be.
- Remove the beans and the lard from the water and put them in a large casserole
- Add the onion, split in quarters
- Bring to a boil and let simmer until the beans barely hold together and the lard fat is completely disolved.
- Take a fraction of the black beans mixture and blend it to a paste
- Put the blend back into the unblended black beans mixture and stir.
- Add the spices
- In a separate pan, lightly fry the garlic in olive oil and add it to the main casserole once the garlic start being fragrant.
- Simmer for 30 more minutes

Enjoy! Great pairing with rice and fried plantains.
