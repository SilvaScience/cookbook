Ingredient:
- one can of Stuffed Marinated vine leaves
- Couscous
- Kale
- Lupini beans
- Seasoning of your choice (recommended Madras Curry powder)
- optionnnal: dried tomatoes and peppers
Preparation:
- Go to Oda and get the ingredients. Spite at the bourgeoisie
- Chop up the kale and mix with seasoning and couscous in a sealable tupperware container
- add water up to level of couscous
- Warm up in microwave for 5 minutes
- mix
- add sutffed vine leaves and some lupini beans and other ingredients.

Enjoy!
