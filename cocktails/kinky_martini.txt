Recipe of a olive oil infuse martini (extra extra dirty ;))
Ingredients
	-whole bottle of gin (preferebly something herbal)
	-a cup a good olive oil
	-olive for garnish


directions:
	for the olive oil infuse gin
		-combine de gin and olive oil. let it sit for a couble of hours to infuse (up to a day)
		-freeze the mixture to separate de fat and the gin
		-pass the mixture trough a coffee filter
		-you now have a olive oil infuse gin !

	for the martini
		-combine a 2 oz of olive oil infuse gin, 1/2 oz of white vermouth
		-stir for 30 second on ice (do not shake!)
		-serve up in a chilled glasse with a olive for garnish
	
** you can add olive brine to taste, I usally go for 1/2 oz **
